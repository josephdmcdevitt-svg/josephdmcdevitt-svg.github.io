Featured Project

Active Development

AI-Powered Private Equity Deal Sourcing Engine

An automated system that scans business-for-sale marketplaces, ingests listings, scores opportunities against institutional investment criteria, and surfaces the highest-priority deals in a live dashboard.

Problem

Traditional private equity deal sourcing is manual, relationship-dependent, and slow. Analysts spend significant time scanning fragmented listing platforms, copying data into spreadsheets, and applying inconsistent evaluation criteria across opportunities. High-quality deals get buried in noise. By the time a team identifies and qualifies a promising target, the window to act has often narrowed or closed entirely.

Objective

Build a system that eliminates the manual bottleneck in early-stage deal origination. The goal is to continuously monitor acquisition marketplaces, automatically extract and normalize listing data, apply a repeatable scoring methodology, and deliver a ranked pipeline of opportunities — reducing time-to-decision from days to minutes.

Solution Overview

The engine operates as an end-to-end pipeline: source listings from business-for-sale platforms, ingest and normalize raw data, classify by EBITDA band and industry, score against weighted investment criteria, prioritize the opportunity set, and display results in a real-time dashboard with configurable alerts.

How It Works

  • Data Collection — Automated scrapers pull listings from target marketplaces on a recurring schedule, capturing financials, asking price, industry, location, and seller notes
  • Processing — Raw listings are cleaned, deduplicated, and normalized. EBITDA is extracted or estimated. Valuation multiples are calculated from asking price and reported earnings
  • Scoring Engine — Each deal is evaluated against weighted criteria including recurring revenue indicators, owner dependency signals, industry attractiveness, EBITDA band, and valuation multiple relative to sector benchmarks
  • Storage — Processed deals are stored in a structured database with full audit trail, enabling historical analysis and trend tracking across the pipeline
  • Dashboard — A live interface displays the ranked deal pipeline with filtering by score, EBITDA range, industry, and geography. Visual indicators flag top-tier opportunities
  • Alerts — Configurable notifications trigger when new listings exceed defined score thresholds, ensuring high-priority deals are reviewed immediately

Scoring Methodology

The scoring model assigns weighted values across five dimensions:

  • Recurring Revenue — Higher scores for subscription, contract, or retainer-based revenue models that indicate cash flow predictability
  • Owner Dependency — Lower scores for businesses where operations are concentrated in the owner, indicating transition risk
  • Industry Filter — Sector-specific multipliers reflecting target industry preferences and macro tailwinds
  • EBITDA Attractiveness — Band-based scoring that prioritizes deals within a defined earnings range appropriate for the buyer's capital base
  • Valuation Multiple — Comparative scoring against sector median multiples to identify relative value

Output and Value

  • Reduces initial deal screening time from hours of manual review to automated, continuous monitoring
  • Standardizes evaluation criteria across all opportunities, eliminating inconsistency in early-stage filtering
  • Surfaces deals that would otherwise be missed in high-volume listing environments
  • Provides structured data for faster decision-making and cleaner handoff to due diligence
