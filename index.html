Featured Project

Active Development

AI-Powered Private Equity Deal Sourcing Engine

An automated system that scans business-for-sale marketplaces, ingests listings, scores opportunities against investment criteria, and surfaces the highest-priority deals in a live dashboard.

Problem

PE deal sourcing is manual and slow. Analysts waste hours scanning fragmented platforms, copying data into spreadsheets, and applying inconsistent criteria. Good deals get buried. By the time a target is qualified, the window to act has often closed.

Objective

Eliminate the manual bottleneck in deal origination. Continuously monitor marketplaces, auto-extract and normalize listings, apply repeatable scoring, and deliver a ranked pipeline — reducing time-to-decision from days to minutes.

How It Works

Data Collection

Automated scrapers pull listings on a recurring schedule — financials, asking price, industry, location, and seller notes

Processing

Raw listings are cleaned, deduplicated, and normalized. EBITDA extracted. Valuation multiples calculated

Scoring Engine

Each deal evaluated against weighted criteria — revenue quality, owner dependency, industry, EBITDA band, multiples

Storage

Structured database with full audit trail for historical analysis and pipeline trend tracking

Dashboard

Live ranked pipeline with filtering by score, EBITDA range, industry, and geography. Visual flags for top deals

Alerts

Automated notifications when new listings exceed score thresholds — high-priority deals flagged immediately

Scoring Methodology

Recurring Revenue

Higher scores for subscription, contract, or retainer-based models indicating cash flow predictability

Owner Dependency

Lower scores where operations are concentrated in the owner, indicating transition risk

Industry Filter

Sector-specific multipliers reflecting target industry preferences and macro tailwinds

EBITDA Attractiveness

Band-based scoring prioritizing deals within a defined earnings range for the buyer's capital base

Valuation Multiple

Comparative scoring against sector median multiples to identify relative value

Output & Value

  • Screening time reduced from hours to continuous automated monitoring
  • Consistent evaluation criteria across all opportunities
  • Surfaces deals that would otherwise be missed in high-volume environments
  • Structured data for faster decisions and cleaner DD handoff
