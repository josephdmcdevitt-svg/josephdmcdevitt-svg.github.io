AI-Powered Private Equity Deal Sourcing Engine

An automated system that scans business-for-sale marketplaces, scores opportunities against investment criteria, and surfaces the highest-priority deals in a real-time dashboard.

Problem

PE deal sourcing is manual and slow. Analysts waste hours scanning fragmented platforms, copying data into spreadsheets, and applying inconsistent criteria. Good deals get buried before they're even reviewed.

Solution

An end-to-end pipeline that continuously monitors marketplaces, auto-extracts and normalizes listings, classifies by EBITDA band and industry, scores against weighted criteria, and delivers a ranked pipeline — reducing time-to-decision from days to minutes.

How It Works

Data Collection

Automated ingestion of listings — financials, asking price, industry, location, and seller notes

Processing

Listings cleaned, deduplicated, normalized. EBITDA extracted. Valuation multiples calculated

Scoring Engine

Weighted evaluation — revenue quality, owner dependency, industry, EBITDA band, multiples

Dashboard

Live ranked pipeline with filtering by score, EBITDA range, industry, geography, and alerts

Key Highlights

  • Screening time reduced from hours to continuous automated monitoring
  • Consistent, repeatable evaluation criteria across all opportunities
  • Surfaces deals that would be missed in high-volume environments
  • Structured data for faster decisions and cleaner DD handoff

Pharmacy M&A Intelligence Platform

A full-stack acquisition targeting dashboard that identifies, scores, and tracks independent pharmacy acquisition targets using NPI registry data, Medicare Part D claims, and U.S. Census demographics.

Problem

Pharmacy acquisition teams manually sift through fragmented public databases to find independent targets. There's no centralized tool that combines provider registry data, prescription volume, demographics, and competitive density into a single scoring system.

Solution

An automated pipeline that ingests 70,000+ pharmacy records from CMS, classifies them as chain vs. independent, enriches with Medicare claims and Census demographics, scores acquisition attractiveness, and surfaces top targets in a filterable dashboard with a built-in deal pipeline tracker.

How It Works

Data Ingestion

Downloads and processes the full CMS NPI registry — 70K+ pharmacies across all 50 states

Classification

Regex engine matches 50+ chains. Flags institutional pharmacies. Clusters multi-location operators

Scoring

Composite 0–100 score combining Rx volume, competition density, demographics, and market signals

Dashboard & Pipeline

Filterable targets, interactive map, deal tracker with 8-stage funnel, and CSV export for outreach

Key Highlights

  • 70,000+ pharmacies ingested and classified across all 50 states
  • Multi-source enrichment: NPI registry, Medicare Part D claims, U.S. Census demographics
  • Change detection system tracks new registrations, deactivations, and field-level updates
  • Full deal pipeline tracker from prospect through closing with contact management
  • Dockerized full-stack deployment — FastAPI, React, PostgreSQL in one command

AI-Powered Travel Planner

A personalized travel recommendation engine covering 326 cities across 84 countries. Takes users through a guided questionnaire and generates custom multi-city itineraries with route maps and budget breakdowns.

Problem

Planning multi-city international trips is overwhelming. Travelers spend hours researching destinations, comparing costs, checking seasons, and building itineraries — often ending up with generic recommendations that don't match their actual preferences.

Solution

A 9-step preference wizard feeds into a 30+ factor scoring algorithm that evaluates all 326 cities against the user's budget, interests, comfort level, and travel style — then auto-generates optimized multi-city itineraries with day-by-day activities, route maps, and cost estimates.

How It Works

Preference Wizard

9-step questionnaire captures budget, travel style, interests, comfort level, and deal-breakers

Matching Engine

30+ factor algorithm scores 326 cities on budget, season, safety, language, interests, and more

Smart Routing

Selects optimal countries and cities, allocates days by richness, minimizes travel time between stops

Itinerary & Budget

Day-by-day activities, interactive route map, full cost breakdown by city with accommodation and transport

Key Highlights

  • 326 cities, 84 countries — each with 15+ data fields, curated activities, neighborhoods, and local cuisine
  • 30+ factor scoring algorithm balancing budget, season, safety, language, dietary needs, and interests
  • Intelligent multi-country routing with day allocation proportional to interest richness
  • Explore map with all 326 cities plotted, color-coded by region with hover detail
  • Tools: city comparison, budget calculator, top-ranked lists, and "Surprise Me" random generator
